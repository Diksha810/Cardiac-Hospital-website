Homecare Services

Our homecare services are designed to bring quality healthcare to the comfort of your home. Whether recovering from illness, managing a chronic condition, or needing assistance with daily activities, our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is here to support you. Services include: Nursing Care: Skilled nursing services such as wound care, medication management, and post-operative care. Personal Care: Assistance with activities of daily living (ADLs) such as bathing, grooming, and dressing..

laboratory Services

In a cardiac hospital, laboratory services are specialized to diagnose and monitor heart conditions. Key services include: Cardiac Biomarker Testing: Measures substances like troponin and BNP to detect heart damage. Lipid Profile: Assesses cholesterol levels to evaluate cardiovascular risk. Electrolyte Panel: Checks levels of essential electrolytes affecting heart function. Coagulation Studies: Evaluates blood clotting, crucial for managing conditions like atrial fibrillation. Renal Function Tests: Monitors kidney function.
